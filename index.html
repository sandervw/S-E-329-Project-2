Math Games

The math games available at PED are built to make math an interesting subject, even if you don't like numbers. Math is an important subject, with a wide range of complexity, so our games have scaling difficulties, to simply teach numbers, or maybe to teach even the most difficult of differential equations. Learning Calculus has never been more fun!

Writing Games

Writing in modern times has been completely replaced by typing. If you know how to type quickly, accurately, you can make it through even the most difficult of english classes. Our time based typing game will improve your speed, accuracy, and word choice by forcing you to make quick and important decisions.

Reading Games

Reading about another persons journey can be boring, and make you feel inferior. We developed a reading game that forces you to read quickly, and comprehend what the story is trying to say. Failing to read quickly enough will force you to make an uninformed decision, so in order to achieve victory, you will need to learn to read flawlessly.
